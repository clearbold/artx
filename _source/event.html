Workshop with artist Jason Middlebrook Toggle as a favorite

ICA Boston

Sat, May 3, 10:15-1:15

A featured artist in the ICA’s collection, Jason Middlebrook commonly uses organic motifs and materials to explore the relationship between nature and culture. Join him for this special introduction to his creative process, tools, and materials; working with wood planks provided by the artist, participants will prepare works to take home at a later date. All skill levels welcome.

A limited number of full scholarships are available for Boston Public School teachers with advance registration. For information, contact Leah Kandel at lkandel@icaboston.org.
